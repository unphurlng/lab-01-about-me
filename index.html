Section I - Blurb

I'm Heather. I moved to Portland, Oregon five years ago. I have two adult boys and two minor girls. I have known that I wanted to learn to be a software developer for more than four years. I took a Basic computer class my senior year of high school and loved it, but that was a very long time ago (some 40 years ago). At that time girls were not encouraged to follow such passions and it was not something I even considered.

I have thought a million and one times since then that I would have been good at it (or at least had an affinity for it). I learned about coding bootcamps a little over four years ago and decided immediately that I must.
